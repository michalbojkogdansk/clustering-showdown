Business Scenario KMeans DBSCAN
"Group customers into 5 segments for our campaign"✅ Perfect⚠️ Possible
"Find unusual transactions in our payment data"❌ Can't detect outliers✅ Built-in
"We don't know how many groups exist"❌ Must specify k✅ Auto-discovers
"Process 10M records in real-time"✅ Fast (O(n·k))⚠️ Slow without index
"Map delivery zones from GPS data"⚠️ Only circular zones✅ Any shape
"Results must be reproducible every run"⚠️ Depends on init✅ Deterministic
"Non-technical stakeholders need to understand it"✅ Simple concept⚠️ ε and minPts confuse people
